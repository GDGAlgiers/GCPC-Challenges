# Morgan's Horse Hunt (Medium)

## Description 
In the wild west, Morgan finds himself in need of exactly L horses after losing his beloved companions. With a limited budget in hand that he must exhaust entirely without leaving a cent behind, Morgan embarks on a quest to replenish his stable. Your task is to assist Morgan in finding all possible combinations of horses he can purchase within his budget constraints.

## Input 
- An array of integers representing the prices of available horses.
- An integer representing Morgan's budget.
- An integer L representing the exact number of horses Morgan needs to purchase.


## Examples

### Example 1
Input: `[41, 2, 39, 4, 8, 13, 46, 41, 23] 77 4`
Output: `[[2, 13, 23, 39]]`


### Example 2 
Input: `[17, 26, 24, 34, 15, 9, 7, 49, 6, 33, 27, 20, 1, 44, 24] 55 5`
Output: `[[1, 6, 7, 15, 26], [1, 6, 7, 17, 24], [1, 6, 9, 15, 24]]`


## Constraints
- L >= 2
